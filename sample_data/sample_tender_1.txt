GOVERNMENT TENDER NOTICE

Tender Reference: EU-EN-2024-001
Publication Date: 2024-01-15
Contracting Authority: Ministry of Energy
Address: 123 Government Street, Brussels, Belgium

Subject: Development of Renewable Energy Infrastructure

Description: The Ministry of Energy is seeking proposals for the development of renewable energy infrastructure including solar and wind power installations. This project aims to increase the country's renewable energy capacity by 50% over the next five years.

The successful contractor will be responsible for:
- Design and implementation of solar photovoltaic systems
- Installation of wind turbines in designated areas
- Grid connection and energy storage solutions
- Environmental impact assessments
- Community engagement and consultation

Estimated Budget: €2,500,000

Eligibility Requirements:
- EU registered company
- Minimum 5 years experience in renewable energy projects
- ISO 14001 environmental management certification
- Financial capacity of at least €1,000,000
- Valid insurance coverage of at least €5,000,000
- Previous experience with government contracts

Tender Deadline: 2024-03-15 17:00 CET

Contact Information:
Name: Dr. Marie Dubois
Email: marie.dubois@ministry.gov
Phone: +32 2 123 4567

Additional Information:
This tender is part of the European Green Deal initiative and supports the EU's goal of carbon neutrality by 2050. The successful contractor will be required to provide detailed environmental impact assessments and community engagement plans.

Evaluation Criteria:
- Technical proposal (40%)
- Financial proposal (30%)
- Environmental impact (20%)
- Community engagement (10%)

Submission Requirements:
- Technical proposal (max 50 pages)
- Financial proposal with detailed cost breakdown
- Company credentials and certifications
- Previous project references
- Environmental impact assessment methodology

The contract will be awarded for a period of 3 years with possible extension for 2 additional years based on performance evaluation.
