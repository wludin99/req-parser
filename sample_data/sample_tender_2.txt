Subject: Smart City Transportation Infrastructure

Description: The Department of Transport is inviting tenders for the development of smart city transportation infrastructure including intelligent traffic management systems, electric vehicle charging networks, and integrated public transport solutions.

Project Scope:
- Installation of smart traffic lights with AI-powered optimization
- Deployment of electric vehicle charging stations across the city
- Integration of public transport systems with mobile applications
- Implementation of real-time traffic monitoring and management
- Development of sustainable transport policies

Estimated Budget: £1,800,000

Eligibility Criteria:
- UK registered company or EU company with UK subsidiary
- Minimum 3 years experience in smart city projects
- ISO 9001 quality management certification
- Financial turnover of at least £500,000 in the last 3 years
- Technical expertise in IoT and smart city technologies
- Previous experience with government contracts
